Growlytic Account Deletion

At Growlytic, we value your privacy and give you complete control over your account and personal information. You can permanently delete your account directly from within the Growlytic mobile application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Growlytic mobile app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. A confirmation alert will appear.
  6. Tap Delete to permanently delete your account.

Once you confirm, your account deletion request is processed automatically.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Growlytic account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your registered email address.
  • Your saved preferences and settings.
  • Your data associated with your Growlytic account.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required by law, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or other legitimate legal obligations.

Deletion Processing Time

Your account deletion request begins immediately after confirmation. Complete removal of your account and associated personal data may take up to 30 days.
